Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe features layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and gooey cheese. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups béchamel sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, cook ground beef with onion and garlic until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the beef mixture and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Spread a layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish. Add a layer of noodles, followed by béchamel sauce and cheese.
  6. Repeat layers until ingredients are used up, ending with a layer of cheese on top.
  7. Bake for 30-40 minutes, or until bubbly and golden brown on top. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
